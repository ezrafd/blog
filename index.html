Hi. So what is this? This is a way for you to see into my mind. That is not an easy thing to do, but language is the best method we have so far to understand someone’s experience. I always hated writing. Unfortunately, I have come to realize how incredible of a tool it is. As a computer scientist, I think about our brains as computer memory and our writing as writing to disk. Writing to disk not only keeps it in long term storage but also sets a base of what makes the computer unique and provides new direction if it is information of a certain type, like an app. (If I have gone too far into the analogy, I am saying the app is when like we write with a purpose.) Oh, and it allows us to learn from people we’ve never met across countries and across generations. No wonder it changed the world.
